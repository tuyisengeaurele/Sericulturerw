Sericulture

Sericulture, is the practice of rearing silkworms for silk production. This process supports sustainable farming and creates high-value silk products.

With advancements in technology, sericulture has become more efficient, allowing farmers to monitor silkworm health, optimize feeding schedules, and increase production yields, which has led to greater profitability.

Sericulture Rwanda is set to transform Rwanda's silk industry by equipping farmers with the knowledge and tools needed to enhance productivity. By integrating modern technologies like IoT for farm monitoring and offering comprehensive e-learning resources, the platform will empower farmers to adopt best practices, improve silkworm health, and achieve higher yields.
